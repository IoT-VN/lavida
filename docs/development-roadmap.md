# Development Roadmap - La Vida Landing Page

## Project Overview
This roadmap outlines the planned phases and milestones for the La Vida landing page implementation. The project follows an agile approach with weekly sprints and continuous delivery.

- **Status**: Planning
- **Focus**: Foundation and architecture

### Phase 2: Core Implementation (Week 2-3)
- **Duration**: 2 weeks
- **Status**: Upcoming
- **Focus**: Landing page components and features

### Phase 3: Testing & Optimization (Week 4)
- **Duration**: 1 week
- **Status**: Upcoming
- **Focus**: Quality assurance and performance

### Phase 4: Deployment & Monitoring (Week 5)
- **Duration**: 1 week
- **Status**: Upcoming
- **Focus**: Production deployment and monitoring

## Risk Assessment

### High Risk Items
- **Performance Optimization**: May require significant refactoring
- **Responsive Design**: Complex layouts may need multiple iterations
- **Third-party Dependencies**: External services may cause issues

### Mitigation Strategies
- Early performance testing and optimization
- Progressive enhancement approach
- Backup implementations for external services
